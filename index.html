Introduction

The game is about air pollution, and the goal of it is to fix the earth. What happens is that you start out with some money, some knowledge on the topic, and some followers. The air pollution level (Based on the "Air Quality Health Index" in Hong Kong) is high, and, in order to survive, you must use the proposals given to you to earn money, gain followers, and even directly affecting the pollution increase and decrease levels to get the pollution to zero. Good luck!

How To Play

You have to make several choices to play the game. First of all, go into the game tab. Then, click start the game.

"This will be the hardest choice you will ever make throughout the entire gamepIay, if I'm gonna be brutally honest" - Hyder

You should see a proposal and several buttons should pop up. These buttons will be your "Options", while the proposal is what the options are reffering to. Select the option that you think will seem the best, with the "best" being something that will help the environment.

The goal of the game is to get your pollution level really low, while the public awareness should be high.

Be careful, because your Pollution Increase per turn will add to your pollution. And once the pollution fills up the progress bar, you're dead! Another way to die would be to go bankrupt, so don't do that either. Good luck!


About Us
